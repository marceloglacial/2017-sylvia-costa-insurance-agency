About Us

Welcome and thank you for visiting our Website. We would like to take a minute of your time and explain who we are and what we are all about. Sylvia F. Costa Ins Agency is a family owned and operated Independent Insurance Agency; offering automobile, homeowners and business insurance. The agency has been owned and operated by Sylvia and Fernando Costa since 2000.

As an independent insurance agency, we are not obligated to any one company and represent only top financially sound companies. By working with a variety of insurance providers, we can select a company according to your individual needs and not the needs of a single company.

Our partnership with solid and reputable insurers, we promise you the correct policy for you and your business.

To make sure all our customers needs are understood all our staff speaks English, Portuguese and Spanish. We are focused on maintaining relationships with our clients.
